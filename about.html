Hello! I'm Gayathri

I'm an AI/ML Engineer who loves turning complex problems into elegant solutions. With a freshly minted Master's in Computer Science from UMass Amherst (perfect 4.0 GPA!), I bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

My journey spans from building healthcare AI systems that help patients get better care, to researching how we can make language models safer and more personalized. I thrive at the intersection of innovation and impact.
